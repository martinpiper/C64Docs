Issue 15, 1985 : Disk Drive 4

Model: 1541

RE: COMMON FAILURE SYPTOM: DRIVE RUNS CONTINUOSLY.

TROUBLESHOOOTING GUIDE:

1)Check system RESET - 6502, Pin 40
2)Check system RINS
3)Check 7417 driver - UD2
4)Check R/W - Must be LOW (Not above 4.0V)
5)Check for cold solder joint at J7 (Newtronics only)
6)Check MTR signal - UC2, 6522
7)Check _MTR signal - UC1, PLA

NUMEROUS CALLS HAVE COME IN TO THE TECH HOT LINE CONCERNING THIS FAILURE INDICATION.

PLEASE REFER TO THIS CHECK LIST BEFORE CALLING FOR HELP.
