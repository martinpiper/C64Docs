Currently, there are 3 versions of the C-128 board:
Rev. 6
Rev. 7-01 150 nS RAM
Rev. 7-02 200 nS RAM

All boards are interchangeable and operationally identical. Therefore, they are all stocked under the same part number: 310279-02. However, the diffrences must be considered when repairing to the component level. The updated schematic reflects those diffrences.

Also, some artwork changes were required to accomplish the modifictions required to pass FCC testing. Those cuts and jumpers involved will be detailed in the next Techtopic mailing.
